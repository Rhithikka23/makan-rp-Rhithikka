Welcome to Makan@RP

Makan@RP is an AI-powered chatbot platform designed to help Republic Polytechnic students and staff find food options on campus efficiently. Our intelligent recommendation system learns your preferences and saves you time by suggesting the best dining options based on proximity, availability, and your taste.

Key Features

  • Smart Recommendations – AI learns your food preferences
  • Real-time Availability – Know what's available right now
  • Location-based Suggestions – Find food near you on campus
  • Time-saving – No more indecision about where to eat
